✦ The Story

An Adventure Written
in the Stars

In the world of Mirai, ancient forests are fading and the golden light that once guided travelers has grown dim. You are Kai — a young explorer with a mysterious paw-marked compass who must journey through forgotten realms alongside Luna, your spirited orange cat companion.

Together, you'll unravel the secrets of the Atelier — a legendary workshop said to hold the power to restore balance to a world on the brink of eternal twilight.

✦ Core Features

Everything That Makes Mirai
Truly Magical

🌲

Living Ecosystems

Every forest breathes. Weather, seasons, and animal behaviors shift dynamically, creating a world that feels genuinely alive around you.

🐱

Companion Bonding

Your companions learn from your journey. Luna evolves, unlocks new abilities, and even remembers your choices across adventures.

🏺

Ancient Crafting

Discover forgotten recipes passed down through village elders. Combine rare flora, minerals, and moonlight to forge legendary items.

🗺️

Procedural Secrets

Hidden caves, mysterious shrines, and rare encounters are seeded uniquely in each playthrough — no two adventures are the same.

🎨

Painterly Visuals

Hand-painted in a warm, studio-inspired aesthetic. Every scene feels like a frame from an animated film you never want to end.

🌙

Night Mysteries

As day fades, a parallel world awakens. Moonlit quests, spirit companions, and nocturnal secrets reveal the deeper story of Mirai.
